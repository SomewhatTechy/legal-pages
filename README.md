# Legal Pages

Centralized legal pages (privacy policy, terms of service, etc.) for all web properties and applications operated by Ecommerce Shop Cart LLC.

## Contents

- `privacy.html` — Privacy Policy. Brand-neutral, GDPR/CCPA-aware. Used as the privacy policy URL for Meta, Pinterest, Google, and other platform app registrations across all brands (The Anastasia Method, Sweet Hustle, etc.).

## Hosting

Deployed via Cloudflare Pages from this GitHub repository. Pushes to `main` auto-deploy.

## Adding a New Brand

The privacy policy explicitly references "The Anastasia Method, Sweet Hustle, and related digital ebook brands". When adding a new brand, update Section 1 (intro paragraph) accordingly and bump the "Last updated" date.
